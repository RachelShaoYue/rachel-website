Nanyang Museum

Wancheng District, Nanyang, Henan, China

Nanyang Museum is near a courtyard and three other museums. The museum is full of historical-cultural relics and materials; its focus is the development of Nanyang and the city's historical figures.

Neixiang County Yamen

Neixiang County, Nanyang, Henan, China

Neixiang County Yamen is a feudal government official residence built in the eighth year of Yuan Dade. There are six groups of courtyards and 85 houses, and it has a scene of a Qiny Dynasty county.

Nanyang Museum of Han Dynasty Stone Carving

Wolong District, Nanyang, Henan, China

The Nanyang Museum of Han Dynasty Stone Carving is the first museum in China that is specialized in the collection and research of Han Dynasty stone carvings. It has a collection of over 1,300 stone carvings. The two famous masterpieces are Tianlu Bixie and Xu Ahqu Tomb Stone Carving of the Eastern Han Dynasty.
