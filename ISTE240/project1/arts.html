Meng Chao

Meng Chao was born in 1967 in Nanyang. He discovered jade carving from Wu Yuanquan, and then he became the fourth Chinese jade carving master

He applied modern advanced science and technology to traditional art and crafts.

Chen Peigiu

Chen Peiqiu, born in Nanyang in 1923, is one of the most famous ink wash artists. She was fascinated with traditional Chinese art, and she studied Chinese artworks to develop her signature color. In many of her artworks, she included flowers and birds because she admired paintings with those elements from the Song Dynasty.
