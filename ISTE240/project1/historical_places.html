Wuhou Temple

Wolong District, Nanyang, Henan, China

Wuhou Temple is the place to honor Zhuge Liang, a famous statesman and strategist in the Three Kingdoms period. The temple is decorated with flowers, and there are some ruins and relics near the temple to see.

District Magistrate Office

Wancheng District, Nanyang, Henan, China

District Magistrate Office is a government office built from the Yuan Dynasty to the eighth year of the Yuan Dynasty. It has a dungeon and torture exhibition to see and explore.

Shedian Ancient Town

Sherqi County, Nanyang, Henan, China

Shedian Ancient Town was built in the Eastern Han Dynasty; it is a small ancient commercial town. There are well preserved ancient buildings and structures. The craftsmanship is very valued and delicate in the area; there is a variety of carved materials, including wood, stone, brick, and glass. It also has many old shops and houses from the Ming and Qing Dynasties.
