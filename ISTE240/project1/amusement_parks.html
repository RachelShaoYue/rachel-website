Xixia Dinosaur Relics Park

Xixia County, Nanyang, Henan, China

Xixia Dinosaur Relics Park is a gigantic treasure-like place with a collection of dinosaur skeletons, fossils, and eggs. It also has some information about the development of the earth and the life of dinosaurs.

International Jade City

Zhengping County, Nanyang, Henan, China

International Jade City is a popular tourist area; it's renowned for its jade sculptures. It is an international market of jade carving, jewels, and crafts. More than five thousand kinds of jade carvings are produced in the International Jade City, and they are being sold to more than 50 countries and regions.

Nanyang People's Park

Wancheng District, Nanyang, Henan, China

Nanyang People's Park is an artistic park with various plants, gardens, and other natural things. There are also some artificial things, such as bridges, pavilions, and fountains.
