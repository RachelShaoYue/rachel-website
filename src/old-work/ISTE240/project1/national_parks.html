Laojie Ridge

Xixia County, Nanyang, Henan, China

Laojie Ridge is a spectacular scenery of mountains near the Yangtze River and the Yellow River. There are some trails to walk around, and there are also several statues and god stones to see

Dan River Daguanyuan

Xichuan County, Nanyang, Henan, China

Dan River Daguanyuan is a place that has a beautiful harmony of the natural landscape and humanistic landscape. It is known to have a series of influential and historical celebrities growing up in the area.
